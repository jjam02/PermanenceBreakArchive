Do Objects Exist If You Don't Look At Them?

Our Game Has It's Own Answer


In our game, manipulate cubes that both exist and don't when they're not in sight.

Our Goal

We want to continue to create levels, add new features, and integrate a narrative into our game. There are still many features and concepts that have yet to be developed. As a small team of developers, we did all that we could during production of our vertical slice for this game. That being said, we are extremely passionate in seeing this project through, and realizing our shared vision of what we know the game can be.
